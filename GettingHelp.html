Mailing List

pyjs has a very active user mailing list, driven by a helpful and respecting community. We care for equal opportunities and diversity. Currently, we use Google Groups to host the mailing lists.

  • pyjs-users - General mailing list for users and developers.
  • pyjs-devel - Developer specific mailing list, commits and other automated systems report to this list.
